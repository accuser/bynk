# v0.29.4 — Refactor track, increment 4: the `UnitInfo` aggregate

- **Status:** Draft (proposal). **Merging is approval to build.** *(Refactor track item 6 — the
  data-model cleanup that `v0.29.3` was sequenced to make **trivial**. Gate on `v0.29.3` (pipeline
  decomposition) is **satisfied** (merged): the phase + per-unit functions already take the parallel
  maps as explicit params — and the `too_many_arguments` `#[allow]`s they carry are exactly the smell
  this removes. Behaviour-preserving — a data-model change with byte-identical output.)*

## Context — the parallel-maps smell (refs post-`v0.29.3`)

- **Eight** `HashMap<String, _>`, all keyed on the **same** unit name, are produced by the pipeline's
  phase functions and threaded in lockstep (verified, `karnc/src/project.rs`):

  | map | value | produced by |
  |---|---|---|
  | `kinds` | `UnitKind` | `phase_group` (`:467`) |
  | `unit_tables` | `UnitTable` | `phase_symbol_tables` (`:682`) |
  | `unit_uses` | `Vec<String>` | `phase_resolve_uses` (`:702`) |
  | `unit_consumes` | `Vec<String>` | `phase_resolve_consumes` (`:772`) |
  | `unit_flattened` | `HashMap<String, String>` | `phase_resolve_consumes` (`:786`) |
  | `unit_consumes_aliases` | `HashMap<String, String>` | `phase_consumes_aliases` (`:965`) |
  | `exports_visibility` | `HashMap<String, Visibility>` | `phase_validate_type_exports` (`:1120`) |
  | `unit_file_index` | `FileDeclIndex` | `phase_file_index` (`:1417`) |

  *(29.3 surfaced two more per-unit maps the pre-split proposal missed — `unit_flattened` and
  `unit_file_index` — both threaded into the same consumer helpers; they fold in too.)*
- The "**all these maps share one keyset**" invariant lives only in the programmer's head and in the
  hand-written unwraps: `kinds.get(name).unwrap()` / `unit_tables.get(name).unwrap()` recur at
  `:788`/`:974`/`:1130`/`:2130` and `:1136`/`:1241`/`:2131` — **16** of the **49** `.get(name)` lookups
  on these maps are `.unwrap()`/`.expect(...)`. A typo or missed insertion is a silent panic, not a
  compile error.
- One record per unit — `HashMap<String, UnitInfo>` — makes the invariant **structural**: a single
  lookup yields `.kind` / `.table` / `.uses` / `.consumes` / `.flattened` / `.aliases` / `.exports` /
  `.file_index` as fields, and the per-column unwraps vanish.

## Builds on (verified)

- **`v0.29.3` already gave the consumers explicit signatures — and flagged the smell.** The per-unit
  steps `compose_unit_symbols` (`:1538`), `merge_consumed_exports` (`:1450`), `check_unit_files`
  (`:1782`), and `emit_unit` (`:1607`) take five-to-eight `&HashMap<String, _>` params each — so many
  that three carry a `#[allow(clippy::too_many_arguments)]` (`merge_consumed_exports`, `emit_unit`,
  `check_unit_files`). **Removing those `too_many_arguments` allows by collapsing the map params into
  one `&UnitInfo` is this increment's concrete, checkable success metric.** *(The `type_complexity`
  allows on the producers `phase_group`/`phase_resolve_consumes`/`compose_unit_symbols` are
  return-tuple complexity, a separate matter `UnitInfo` does not address — do not expect those to go.)*
- **The phase-8 loop is now small and delegating.** `for (name, indices) in &groups` (`:2129`)
  re-fetches `kinds.get(name).unwrap()` (`:2130`) and `unit_tables.get(name).unwrap()` (`:2131`) per
  iteration before calling the helpers; iterating `for (name, info) in &unit_info` drops those and
  hands each helper one `&UnitInfo`.
- **`exports_visibility` is genuinely optional** — read as `.unwrap_or(empty_exports)` inside
  `merge_consumed_exports` (`:1477`) and `check_unit_files` (`:1650`), so not every unit has an entry.
  Under `UnitInfo` this becomes a **non-optional field defaulting to an empty map**, which *removes*
  the `unwrap_or` dance rather than preserving it — a small correctness-of-shape win the aggregate
  makes free.
- **`groups` is the keyset authority** — `HashMap<String, Vec<usize>>` (name → file indices), the loop
  driver. It is itself a parallel-by-name map; folding the file indices into `UnitInfo.files` lets the
  back half iterate `unit_info` directly instead of `groups` + side lookups.

## Settled

- **▸ Data-model change, output unchanged.** No observable behaviour, no language surface. The existing
  golden fixtures plus the `v0.29.1` helper pins are the acceptance gate, all green and unedited.
- **▸ The aggregate makes the invariant a type, not a comment.** The goal is to delete the hand-written
  `.expect("every group has a kind")` family by construction — if a unit is in the map, all its facets
  are present because they were assembled together.

## Decisions (open — settle at review)

**[DECISION] Assemble once, after the producer phases — not progressive `Option` fields.** The maps are
produced one per phase function (`kinds` by `phase_group` … `unit_file_index` by `phase_file_index`,
the last per-unit map). Two shapes: *(a)* a `UnitInfo` with `Option<_>` fields filled progressively —
which **reintroduces unwraps** and defeats the purpose; *(b)* keep the phase functions producing their
individual maps as today, then **assemble `HashMap<String, UnitInfo>` once** in the pipeline body at
the seam after the front-half phase calls and before the phase-8 loop (`:2129`), with **all fields
non-optional**. *Rec: (b).* It bounds the blast radius to the consumer helpers — exactly where the
`too_many_arguments` allows sit — and keeps `UnitInfo`'s fields total, so the lookups it serves never
unwrap a facet.

**[DECISION] Scope the swap to the assembly point and the consumers.** The producer phase functions
build individual maps and also do cross-unit reads *while the maps are still being built* (e.g.
`kinds.get(&target).unwrap()` inside `phase_resolve_uses`/`phase_resolve_consumes`); those stay on the
individual maps. *Rec:* the refactor touches the **assembly step + the consumer helpers**
(`compose_unit_symbols`/`merge_consumed_exports`/`collect_unit_methods`/`check_unit_files`/`emit_unit`)
**+ the phase-8 loop body**, swapping their map params for `&UnitInfo` — **not** the producer phase
functions. This is the honest, low-risk boundary that removes the `too_many_arguments` allows without
reaching into half-built state.

**[DECISION] Which facets `UnitInfo` carries.** *Rec:* `kind`, `table`, `uses`, `consumes`,
`flattened`, `aliases`, `exports`, `file_index`, and `files` (the `groups` indices) — the **full**
per-unit-keyed set 29.3 threads, including the two (`unit_flattened`, `unit_file_index`) the pre-split
proposal missed. Folding *all* of them is what actually drops `check_unit_files`/`emit_unit` below the
`too_many_arguments` threshold; a partial fold leaves the allow in place. Transient per-iteration
accumulators stay local.

**None.** No language surface, no `docs/src/spec/` chapter — an internal data-model change only.

## Tests

- **No new golden fixtures.** Output is byte-identical; the existing fixtures plus `v0.29.1`'s pins
  must stay green and unedited. That invariance is the proof.
- **Optional:** a trivial unit test that `UnitInfo` assembly produces one entry per group with all

  and it documents the contract for future readers.

## Diagnostics / Risks

- **Assembly completeness.** The single risk is assembling `UnitInfo` before a map is fully built,
  or omitting a unit — which would turn today's per-column `.unwrap()` into one assembly-time `.expect`.
  Mitigation: assemble strictly **after** the last producer (`phase_file_index`) and drive assembly
  from the `groups` keyset (the authority), so every group yields exactly one `UnitInfo`. The optional
  assembly test above pins this.
- **Borrow shape in the loop.** Iterating `for (name, info) in &unit_info` while the loop body also
  needs `&mut` sinks/accumulators is the usual borrow dance; it is the same shape `v0.29.3` already
  resolved for the carved functions, so no new machinery — just keep `unit_info` borrowed immutably
  and the sinks separate.
- **`exports` default semantics.** Folding `exports_visibility` to a total field that defaults empty
  must reproduce the `unwrap_or(empty_exports)` behaviour exactly (absent entry ≡ no exports). Verify
  the two read sites — `merge_consumed_exports` (`:1477`) and `check_unit_files` (`:1650`) — see an
  empty map, not a panic, for export-less units; a fixture with a unit that exports nothing already
  covers this, so confirm it stays green.
- **Low merge exposure.** As ever under the freeze, no concurrent edits to `project.rs`; the change is
  confined to the consumer helpers + the pipeline body (parent `project.rs`) and lands fast.

## Definition of done

A single `UnitInfo` record (carrying `kind`, `table`, `uses`, `consumes`, `flattened`, `aliases`,
`exports`, `file_index`, `files`) is assembled once after the producer phases and threaded as
`HashMap<String, UnitInfo>` into the consumer helpers and the phase-8 loop; their per-map params
collapse to one `&UnitInfo`, **so the `too_many_arguments` `#[allow]`s on `merge_consumed_exports` /
`emit_unit` / `check_unit_files` are removed** (the increment's checkable success metric); the
per-iteration unwraps and the `unwrap_or(empty_exports)` reads are gone; producer phase functions are
unchanged; **all golden fixtures and the `v0.29.1` pins pass unedited**, plus an optional
assembly-invariant unit test; `cargo build`/`test`/clippy and CI stay green; changelog notes the
aggregate. The implementing PR **deletes this file**.
